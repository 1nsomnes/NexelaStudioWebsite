About Us

Founded by Insomnes or Cedric Claessens, Nexela Studio is a small team officially established in April of 2021. We aim to create fun high quality games while also having fun ourselves! 

Positions

Are you skilled artist or developer? Well, working at Nexela may be the right choice for you! We look for self-driven people ready to have good time and we provide a safe and fun environment for all members of the studio. Currently all code is done for fun and we don't monetize or sell any of our games, so, no salary is offered. However, in the future this may change as demand for our games constantly change!
